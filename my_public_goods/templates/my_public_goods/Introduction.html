Please read the following instructions carefully:

  • Today you will be playing a public goods game.
  • There will be two sessions and six rounds per session, so you will play a total of 12 rounds.
  • You have been randomly assigned to a group. Each group has {{ Constants.players_per_group }} participants. Members of your group are distributed throughout the room and will remain anonymous throughout this experiment. You will remain a part of this group for the duration of the experiment.
  • In each round, you will be given an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }} and will have opportunity to contribute from that endowment to the group.
  • Once all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} members of your group have decided how much to contribute, the sum of contributions to the group will be multiplied by {{ Constants.efficiency_factor }} and divided evenly among all {{ Constants.players_per_group }} group members.
  • Finally, a ranking of group members by the amount contributed to the group will be displayed and each group member will have the chance to sanction other group members for their lack of contribution to the group. It costs {{ Constants.sanction_fee }} to sanction another group member, and the group member being sanctioned will receive a penalty of {{ Constants.sanction_penalty }}.
  • At the end of this experiment, one round will be selected and participants will be paid the amount that they ended that round with.

When you have finished reading the instructions, you may advance to the next page. You will be asked to answer a few questions to confirm that you understand the rules of the game.
